Tournament Calendar
Listings below include all USA Ultimate affiliated tournaments as well as unaffiliated events (not supported or controlled by USAU in any way).  Tournaments sanctioned or operated by USA Ultimate feature a logo and have integration to the USAU event management system, featuring registration, score reporting and participant account history/tracking.  Find a local organization or list your local organization on our Where to Play page.
Unaffiliated events do not display an event logo and are merely listed in the calendar with links to external sites for more information from the organizers.  They can be requested by any USAU account holder from the Event Calendar link.  Send questions to info@hq.usaultimate.org.
