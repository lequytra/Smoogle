Having trouble accessing your existing account? Contact membership@usaultimate.org. Please do not create a new account.


 
NOTES:

Unique email addresses required. The same email address cannot be used for 2 different accounts. Each account must have a unique email address. Tip: with gmail account, you can insert a period/dot (.) anywhere in the username (e.g., user@gmail.com or u.ser@gmail.com or use.r@gmail.com), and all correspondence will be routed to the same account.


Account ID#: Players can be rostered on a team by a team manager using either the email address on the account or by the account ID# (automatically assigned once the account is created, displayed in the upper right corner and sent via email).


Clinics/Camps: If you are registering youth for a Learn to Play, GUM clinic or a licensed/official "USA Ultimate Day Camp," those youth participants do not need a membership just a free USA Ultimate account (for each participant) in order to be registered.


USA Ultimate memberships are not refundable nor transferable. All USA Ultimate memberships are based on the calendar year (January 1 – December 31). Any memberships purchased through November 30 will expire on December 31 of the current year. Memberships for the following year become available on/around December 1. Members are encouraged to sign up as early as possible to receive the most from their membership.


Questions? Do not hesitate to contact info@hq.usaultimate.org with quesitons!
