University of Maryland, Baltimore County
Performing Arts and Humanities Building
Baltimore, MD

This project brings together the Performing Arts and Humanities Faculty in a new state-of-the-art teaching and performance facility. A two-phase project, this new facility includes a 275-seat Proscenium Theatre with a flytower and flexible forestage, a 350-seat Concert Hall, a Black Box Theatre, Dance Studio Theatres, a recital hall, recording studio, rehearsal studios, music technology labs, numerous classrooms, offices and informal gathering spaces. Grimm + Parker Architects is the Associate Architect. (160,000 g.s.f.)> Baltimore Sun, August 2014
