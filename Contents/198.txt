Event Calendar
Here  you can find a complete listing of events.  Both USA Ultimate affiliated events as well as unaffiliated events (not supported or controlled by USAU in any way) are included.
Events sanctioned or run by USA  Ultimate (accompanied by a logo) will have full access to our registration and event management  system and are integrated with our website, score reporting features and member accounts.
Unaffiliated events (which do not display an event logo) can be listed in the calendar but will not have  access to the event management tools and should instead link to an external site for more information from the organizers.  To list an unaffiliated event in the calendar, login to your USAU account and select the Event Calendar link.
To find a local organization or to list your local organization, visit our Where to Play page.
Questions? Contact <info@hq.usaultimate.org>
